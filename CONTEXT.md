# Seersware

Seersware is a Montréal-based IT consulting firm that delivers scoped, outcome-oriented engagements to enterprise clients. The firm positions as a transformation partner, not a staff augmentation provider.

## Language

**Engagement**:
A scoped consulting project with a defined outcome, delivered by Seersware for a Client. Time-bounded and outcome-framed.
_Avoid_: mandate, contract, assignment, placement — these imply an employment or subordination relationship.

**Client**:
An organization that engages Seersware for a Service Offering.
_Avoid_: employer, payor — these imply a principal-agent employment relationship.

**Service Offering**:
A named, outcome-framed consulting service that Seersware delivers. There are four: DevSecOps Pipeline Implementation, AI Adoption Program, Cloud Migration, Engineering Leadership Advisory.
_Avoid_: skill, capability, resource, profile — these describe a person, not a firm's deliverable.

**Discovery Call**:
The first structured conversation between Seersware and a prospective Client. Booked directly via the Contact page. The goal is to scope a potential Engagement.
_Avoid_: interview, screening — these imply an employment context.

**Principal Consultant**:
The senior practitioner who leads Engagement delivery at Seersware. Not named on the public-facing site.
_Avoid_: founder, owner, employee, resource.
